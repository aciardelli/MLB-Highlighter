# MLB Highlighter

![MLB Highlighter Preview](./assets/mlbhighlighter.png)

## Overview

Watching baseball video is difficult. When you find what you want to watch, you have to click through 3 pages per video. Why can't I just look up a highlight and see it instantly? That's what MLB Highlighter aims to solve.

## URL

www.mlbhighlighter.com

## Inputs

MLB Highlighter will accept both Baseball Savant URLs and natural language queries.

Baseball Savant URLs will be instantly parsed and allow you to view all videos that are available.

Natural language will try to guess what your query would be, instead of you manually making it on Baseball Savant. Then, the videos will be displayed to you with the filters that it assumed you wanted.
> **Note:** Natural language is a work in progress and actively being improved. If your query produces wrong results, I am working on a way to report this.
